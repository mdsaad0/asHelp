# 🎉 Hacktoberfest 2025

[![Hacktoberfest](https://img.shields.io/badge/Hacktoberfest-2025-blue?style=flat-square)](https://hacktoberfest.com/)

Hacktoberfest is a **month-long celebration of open-source software**.  
It encourages developers of all levels to contribute to open-source projects on **GitHub** and **GitLab** during October.  

---

## 📅 Key Dates

- **Registration:** September 15 – October 31, 2025  
- **Contribution Period:** October 1 – October 31, 2025  

> Only pull/merge requests created during October count toward Hacktoberfest.  

---

## 🛠️ How to Participate

1. **Register** on the [official Hacktoberfest website](https://hacktoberfest.com/register/).  
2. **Find Projects** with the `hacktoberfest` label on GitHub/GitLab.  
3. **Contribute** by submitting **high-quality pull/merge requests**.  
4. **Earn Rewards** when your PRs are accepted.  

> ⚠️ PRs labeled as spam or low-effort will **not** count.  

---

## 🎁 Rewards

- **Digital Badge** for registration and completed PRs  
- **Limited-Edition T-shirt** for the first 10,000 participants with 6 accepted PRs  
- **Tree Planting**: Every 6th accepted PR contributes to planting a tree 🌱
